Our Commitment

Aftabunnesa strictly adheres to ethical business-practice. It carries out compliance as a continuous process, and exceeds local legislations to meet ILO convention in terms of employment practice and safety. Its compliance management team monitors and reviews the various activities performed throughout the organization.

Safety For Workers

For the safety to its workers, Aftabunnesa has improved workplace-conditions set up noticeable warning signs in the local language, enforced the usage of safety equipment, and build awareness among the workforce. The work-place has clearly demarcated emergency exits and equipment.
