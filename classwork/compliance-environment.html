Environmental Sustainability is Important

All factory in Aftabunnesa is aware of its obligation to the community, its workers, and the environment in order to create environmental sustainability. As a result, we guarantee resource conservation in accordance with applicable local, national, and international rules and regulations. We strive for.

  • To comply with applicable environmental legislations and regulations.
  • To protect natural resources by utilizing energy and water in an efficient manner.
  • To adopt affordable solutions for reducing pollution from our processes.
  • Motivate and equip all employees to take personal responsibility for environmental protection.

We have highly trained and professionally competent Environmental responsible team who have been discharging their effective governance in the area of Environmental & Chemical related requirements even upcoming environmental challenges. The entire environmental compliance functions are being closely monitored & proper feedback given by Director-Compliance.
