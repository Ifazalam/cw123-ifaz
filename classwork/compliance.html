Our Story

Aftabunnesa group is a 100% export oriented garment and textile company. Equipped with highly modern machineries mostly imported from UK, France, Germany, Japan, Taiwan, China and Singapore, using the latest garments and textile machineries.
We have started in 2002 with textile division and soon we started the garments division with woven lines and then knit lines. We are now a group of companies having a good backward linkage support with our textiles and packaging and very good accessories sourcing divisions.
Its factory address is at Dag. No. 6614, South Mutuail, Jatrabari, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It produces both woven and knits products for both USA and Europe markets. The factory’s range of products ranges from basic t-shirts, polo shirts, jackets, tank tops and shorts/trousers for knits division and all items for kids, denim/twill basic, trousers and cargo pants for woven division. Woven Division was established in March 2010 while Knits Division operated from January 2012.

The Management team is controlled and guided by highly educated, dynamic, trustworthy and determined Garment and Textile related professionals in order to maintain the optimum quality of the products we produced as well as on-time delivery of the shipments. The factory is also manned by highly committed and motivated team of designers, managers, supervisors, quality inspectors and controllers and textile experts to be able to provide customer satisfaction. Our team is working in close coordination with our production to match every order to design specifications and clients’ expectation. We are also geared towards better results of our goods.
