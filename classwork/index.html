Aftabunnesa Group At a Glance

Aftabunnesa Group started its journey in 2002 by its founder and Managing Director with a textile business, and shortly after, we launched the apparel section with woven and subsequently knit lines. With our excellent sourcing divisions for accessories, packaging, and textiles, we are now a collection of businesses with strong backward linkage with 20 years of experience in this sector.

Aftabunnesa Group is a clothing and textile firm that is entirely focused on exports. utilizing the most up-to-date clothing and textile machinery, and the machines are imported from the UK, France, Germany, Japan, Taiwan, China, and Singapore.

For the markets in the USA and Europe, it makes both woven and knit items. The factory produces basic t-shirts, polo shirts, jackets, tank tops, shorts/trousers, denim/twill basics, trousers, and cargo pants for the woven section, as well as all items for men, women and kids.

Garments Divisions.

Our garments divisions are equipped with state of the art machineries specialising in both woven and knit.

Textile

Our Textile unit can produce various kinds of woven fabrics. Textile unit can produce 2.4 million yards of fabric annually. The fabrics types are Twill, canvas, poplin, TC/TT pocketing, etc.

Knitting Division
